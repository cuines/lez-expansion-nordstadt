# Draft Ordinance for the Expansion of the Low Emission Zone (LEZ) to Nordstadt

## Section 1 – Purpose and Legal Basis

1. This ordinance is issued on the basis of § 40 of the Federal Immission Control Act (BImSchG) in conjunction with the 39th Ordinance on the Implementation of the Federal Immission Control Act (39. BImSchV) and the EU Clean Air Directive 2008/50/EC.

2. The purpose of this ordinance is to reduce nitrogen dioxide (NO₂) and particulate matter (PM₁₀) concentrations in the Nordstadt district by extending the existing Low Emission Zone.

## Section 2 – Geographical Scope

The Low Emission Zone is extended to include the following area (hereinafter "Nordstadt LEZ"):

- Bounded in the north by the railway line (track no. 1234)
- In the east by the river "Flussstraße"
- In the south by the "Hauptallee" (city ring road)
- In the west by the "Industriepark" boundary

The exact boundaries are depicted in the attached map (Appendix 1) and correspond to the postal code areas 10115, 10117, 10119 (partial).

## Section 3 – Affected Vehicles

1. The following vehicles are prohibited from entering or staying in the Nordstadt LEZ unless they display a valid green emission sticker (Grüne Plakette):
   - Motor vehicles of classes M1, M2, M3 (passenger cars, buses)
   - Motor vehicles of classes N1, N2, N3 (light and heavy commercial vehicles)
   - Tractors and other self-propelled agricultural or forestry machinery (exempt under Section 5)

2. Vehicles with a yellow (Gelbe Plakette) or red (Rote Plakette) emission sticker are not allowed to enter the zone.

3. Vehicles without any emission sticker are prohibited.

## Section 4 – Implementation Timeline

1. The ordinance shall enter into force on **1 January 2026**.

2. A grace period of six (6) months is granted for vehicles registered to residents and businesses with a principal place of business inside the Nordstadt LEZ. During this period, vehicles with a yellow sticker may still enter the zone.

3. From **1 July 2026**, the full prohibition according to Section 3 applies without exception for non‑green‑sticker vehicles.

## Section 5 – Exemptions

The following vehicles are exempt from the prohibition:

a) Emergency vehicles of police, fire brigade, ambulance and civil protection.
b) Vehicles of the German Armed Forces, Federal Police and customs authorities when on official duty.
c) Vehicles of persons with a severe disability (mark “aG”, “H” or “BI”) registered in the vehicle registration document.
d) Tractors and agricultural machinery used for local agricultural or forestry work.
e) Classic cars (vehicles older than 30 years) registered with an “H” licence plate.
f) Commercial delivery vehicles that serve businesses located inside the Nordstadt LEZ, provided they are registered in the city’s logistics register and comply with Euro 6d‑TEMP or better.

## Section 6 – Enforcement and Penalties

1. Violations of this ordinance constitute an administrative offence.

2. The fine for unauthorised entry or stay in the Nordstadt LEZ is set at €100 for passenger cars and €200 for commercial vehicles.

3. Repeated violations within a calendar year may lead to a fine of up to €500.

## Section 7 – Final Provisions

1. This ordinance shall be published in the official gazette of the city and on the city’s website.

2. The public order office is entrusted with the enforcement of this ordinance.

3. This ordinance enters into force on the day after its publication.
